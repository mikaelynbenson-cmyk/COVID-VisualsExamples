What is an Epidemic?

Flu epidemic relates to the percentage of deaths in a given week caused by influenza and pneumonia

Seasonal Baseline is the normal level that is statistically determined based on data from past flu seasons.

Epidemic threshold is a certain percentage above what is considered normal level for that period.


What is a Pandemic?

The main features of a pandemic are (1) a new strain of virus that has never infected people before, (2) it is on a global scale, and (3) sometimes it is also unusually deadly.
